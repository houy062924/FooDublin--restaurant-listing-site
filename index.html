WELCOME FOODIES

Discover Food!

At the heart of any city, and central to many friendships, food is integral to our daily life. Explore Dublin's rich culinary options through local markets, casual cafe's, or chic restaurants. We want you to be aware of your options and open to exploring new flavours, cultures, and experiences. A perfect way to connect to a place or to another person, we hope you enjoy our handpicked recommendations!

MARKETS

A great way to score fresh food, Dublin's food markets give you a vibrant energetic experience. Check out our favourite's down below.

  • Temple Bar

    Running on Saturdays from 10-14:30, this market at the centre of dublin offers great fresh options, rain or shine. Check them out on facebook!

  • Spencer Dock

    A great place to grab a meal for under a tenner, this market at the Art Park offers a delicious selection of meals from food trucks and local chefs every Wednesday from 11:30-14:00.

  • Smithfeild Outdoor Food Market

    Lovely fresh and handpicked produce adorns these stalls, so make sure to come early to grab the best fruits and vegetables Ireland can offer! Find them on Cowpinger Row every Friday from 11:00-15:00
